whatsup by dustin

I'll look at web pages so you don't have to.

Whatsup monitors web pages for you while you're online and lets you know that they're operational and returning content that is interesting to you. To try it out, add whatsup@jabber.org to your roster and have a blast.

Dependencies

twisted and SQLAlchemy

Install

Just do it.
